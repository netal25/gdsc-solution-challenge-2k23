What is
Temperament?

Temperament refers to the innate characteristics and behavioral tendencies of an individual that are relatively stable across situations and time. It can influence an individual's emotional reactivity, sociability, adaptability, and attention span. Temperament is believed to be partly genetic and partly shaped by environmental factors.

4 Temperaments

Sanguine

The Sanguine temperament is characterized by sociability, optimism, enthusiasm, and such an outlook on life that is generally positive.
